Payment Methods

For your convenience we accept different payment methods; please notice that there might be some restrictions for some of them, so we encourage you to read them carefully to make your dental vacation even easier.

Credit/Debit Card

We accept cards with the Visa or Master Card logo. With this payment method you will be charged an extra 4% transaction fee.

Cash

All transactions are in U.S. Currency

Check

(Personal, travelers, cashiers) there is no extra fee with this payment method.

Wire Transfer
